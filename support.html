Frequently asked questions

A station won't play, or keeps buffering

RadioBulgar always tries the best available stream for a station, but the stream itself is hosted by the radio station, not by us — if their server is temporarily down, playback may fail. Try again later, or pick another station in the meantime.

The station list looks out of date

The station list refreshes automatically, but if you've been offline for a while, pull to refresh or reopen the app to fetch the latest list.

How do I reorder my favourites?

Open the Favourites section, tap the edit icon, then drag stations by their handle to reorder them.

Does RadioBulgar work without an internet connection?

You can browse your last loaded station list offline, but playing a station requires an internet connection since the audio streams from the station's own server.

Does the app collect my personal data?

No personal data is collected. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
