Frequently asked questions

A station won't play, or keeps buffering

RadioBulgar always tries the best available stream for a station, but the stream itself is hosted by the radio station, not by us — if their server is temporarily down, playback may fail. Try again later, or pick another station in the meantime.

The station list looks out of date

The station list refreshes automatically when you start the app fresh. If a station's details still look wrong, open Settings and tap the refresh icon next to "Last updated" to fetch the newest list.

Playback stops, or notifications disappear, when the screen is off

This is usually caused by your phone's battery-saving settings, not RadioBulgar — many manufacturers (Xiaomi, Huawei, Samsung, OnePlus, and others) aggressively restrict apps running in the background. Open your phone's battery settings and disable battery optimization (sometimes called "no restrictions" or "allow background activity") for RadioBulgar. dontkillmyapp.com has exact steps for most phone brands.

How do I reorder my favourites?

Open the Favourites section, tap the edit icon, then drag stations by their handle to reorder them.

Does RadioBulgar work without an internet connection?

You can browse your last loaded station list offline, but playing a station requires an internet connection since the audio streams from the station's own server.

How much data does it use?

Streaming uses your data connection for as long as you're listening, the same as streaming music or video — there's no offline playback. If you're on a limited mobile plan, prefer Wi-Fi when you can.

Is this FM radio, or does it use my phone's radio receiver?

RadioBulgar is an internet radio app — it streams each station live over Wi-Fi or mobile data. It does not use your phone's built-in FM tuner (if it has one), and it won't work without a data connection.

Does the app collect my personal data?

RadioBulgar doesn't require an account and doesn't collect personal information like your name or email. See the Privacy Policy for full details on what is collected and why.
