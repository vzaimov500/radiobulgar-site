Privacy Policy for RadioBulgar

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This privacy policy explains what information RadioBulgar ("the app") collects, why, and how it is used.

Summary

RadioBulgar does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not show ads. The only data collected is anonymous crash and performance data used to keep the app stable.

Information we collect

Crash and performance data

RadioBulgar uses Firebase Crashlytics (provided by Google) to automatically detect and report app crashes. When the app crashes or encounters an error, Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • A randomly generated installation identifier (not linked to your name, email, or any account)

This data is used only to identify and fix bugs. It is not used for advertising, and it is not sold or shared with third parties other than Google, who processes it on our behalf. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles this data.

Radio station data

The list of radio stations, their names, logos, and stream URLs is downloaded from our own server (radioapi.vzaimov.com) each time the app refreshes its station list. This request does not include any personal information — it is a plain, unauthenticated request for a public file. Standard web server logs (such as IP address and request time) may be recorded by our hosting provider for security and reliability purposes, the same as any website you visit.

Radio streams

When you play a station, your device connects directly to that station's own streaming server (operated by the radio station itself, not by us) to receive the audio. We do not see or control what those third-party servers log.

Information we do NOT collect

  • No account or sign-up is required or available
  • No name, email address, or phone number is collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No contacts, photos, or files on your device are accessed
  • No advertising identifiers are used, and the app shows no ads

Data storage

Your favourites, recently played stations, and app settings (such as theme and sleep timer) are stored locally on your device only, and are never transmitted to us. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Children's privacy

RadioBulgar is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this same URL with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, use the contact form on our Support page.
