Privacy Policy for RadioBulgar

REV. 2026-06-19

This privacy policy explains what information RadioBulgar ("the app") collects, why, and how it is used.

Summary

RadioBulgar does not require an account and does not collect personal information. Anonymous app-stability data is always collected to keep the app running smoothly. Anonymous statistics about which stations are popular are off by default and only collected if you opt in — you're asked once, the first time you open the app, and can change your choice at any time in Settings.

Information we collect

App stability data

To detect and fix crashes and keep RadioBulgar stable, the app automatically reports anonymous crash and performance data using a tool provided by Google. This data is not linked to your name, email, or any account. It is used only to identify and fix bugs — it is not used for advertising, and it is not sold or shared with third parties other than Google, who processes it on our behalf. See Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles this data.

Station popularity statistics (optional, opt-in)

With your permission, RadioBulgar can collect anonymous statistics about which stations are played and for how long, using a tool provided by Google, so we can see which stations are most popular. This is off by default — the first time you open the app, you're asked whether to allow it, and you can change your answer at any time in Settings. This data is not linked to your name, email, or any account, is not used for advertising, and is not sold to third parties. It is processed by Google on our behalf — see Google's Privacy Policy for details. If you decline or later turn this off in Settings, no statistics are collected or sent.

Radio station data

The list of radio stations, their names, logos, and stream URLs is downloaded from our own server each time the app refreshes its station list. This request does not include any personal information.

Radio streams

When you play a station, your device connects directly to that station's own streaming server (operated by the radio station itself, not by us) to receive the audio. We do not see or control what those third-party servers log.

Data storage

Your favourites, recently played stations, and app settings (such as theme and sleep timer) are stored locally on your device only, and are never transmitted to us. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Children's privacy

RadioBulgar is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this same URL with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, use the contact form on our Support page.
